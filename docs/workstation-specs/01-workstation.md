# Recommended Workstation Specifications

This section outlines the recommended hardware specifications for a development workstation suitable for robotics projects, including ROS 2, simulation, and AI model training.

## Minimum Specifications:
- **Processor**: Intel Core i7 (9th Gen or newer) / AMD Ryzen 7 (3rd Gen or newer)
- **RAM**: 16 GB DDR4
- **Storage**: 500 GB NVMe SSD
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 or equivalent (for CUDA acceleration)
- **Operating System**: Ubuntu 20.04 LTS or newer

## Recommended Specifications:
- **Processor**: Intel Core i9 (10th Gen or newer) / AMD Ryzen 9 (5th Gen or newer)
- **RAM**: 32 GB DDR4 (or more)
- **Storage**: 1 TB NVMe SSD
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3070 or equivalent (for more powerful CUDA acceleration)
- **Operating System**: Ubuntu 22.04 LTS

## Notes:
- Consider dual-booting or virtualization for Windows/macOS users.
- High-performance GPUs are crucial for deep learning and heavy simulations.
